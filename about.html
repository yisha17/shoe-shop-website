What We Do

Addis shoe Shop and ware house is known as innovators and authorities in our industry. We offer a great variety of on-trend styles for women, men and children in athletic, comfort and dress categories, while providing a delightful and trusted shopping experience for our valued customers. We also offer outstanding values on a wide selection of nationally recognized brands and exclusive private brands.

Our customers mean everything to us. You can count on us to consistently provide quality brands, current styles at affordable prices, and great service to fit the way you shop. Serving today’s busy families is our specialty. Our more than 500 stores feature an open concept design, with inventory that’s visible and accessible – makes it much easier and quicker to choose styles and find correct sizes for the entire family. With two brands in one family, you have more locations to shop, with returns or exchanges accepted at Addis Shoe Shop. You can also earn and use your Rack Room Shoes Rewards Points at both locations, Shop Your Way online, in-store, on our APP or with Curbside Pick-Up.
